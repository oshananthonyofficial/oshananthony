Oshan Anthony

Musical Artist & Creator

Oshan Anthony is a Sri Lankan actor and a Musical artist. He began his acting career at a young age, appearing in short films released on YouTube and featured on IMDb. He is known for his role in Tiara: She Came (2025). With a growing interest in film and performance, Oshan continues to build his experience through independent and short-form projects and also with his new song projects.
